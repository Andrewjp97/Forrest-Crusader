Description of Main Characters

Forrest

Our intrepid adventurer from Succulent Spring, Forrest is a shy, timid person. Chosen by his pears to be the meekest around him, he sets off on his quest to Pith to challenge the other contenders. Whether he will succeed in claiming his crown and taking on Decay is up to you!

Rose

The champion from Sizzling Summer, Rose is the ever cheerful, ever perky girlfriend of Forrest. The two star-crossed lovers are destined to fight each other for the right to challenge Decay, and as the battle draws near, things start to fall apart for each of them. Will Forrest defeat Rose, will Rose defeat Forrest, will either of them be able to Fight Decay if they win? It's up to you to find out!

Decay

The embodiment of evil, the dictator of death, Decay is the worst thing to have come upon the land of Arbor sense the time of the flame. He lays waiting in the heart of the forest, slowly devouring the land of Arbor. He is protected by his goons, the Rot Squad and their leader Captain Shroom. If the land of Arbor is to stand, Decay must fall.
